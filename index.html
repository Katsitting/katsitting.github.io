Services and Fees

My flexible services ensure you and your cats’ needs are met, all while aiming for a stress-free, positive experience for you and your feline friend. If there is a service that you require that is not listed, please don’t hesitate to contact me to discuss your individual requirements.

Please note, the fees are given as guidance and may vary based on your individual circumstances. Please enquire for further details and a tailored quote.
Our standard cat sitting package involves providing standard care and attention to your cats in their home environment while you are away. Per visit, I will ensure fresh food and water is provided as required, and any litter boxes are cleaned. I will be happy to provide photos and updates on your cat’s wellbeing, if you wish.

This service is most suitable for independent outdoor cats that require less attention.
Our extended stay cat sitting package is a premium service designed for cats that need more attention and interaction while their owners are away. This service includes all the essentials of standard cat sitting, such as food, water and litter maintenance, but goes a step further by offer extended stay to spend quality time engaging with your cats. This can include an interactive play session, brushing, and even cuddling if your cat is comfortable with this.

This service is most suitable for cats that crave extra attention or have specific routines that require more dedicated care.
I will happily complete minor tasks free of charge. This can include watering houseplants, bringing in mail and packages, or putting out and pulling in your wheelie bins.
